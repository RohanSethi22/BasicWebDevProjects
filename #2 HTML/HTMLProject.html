Supers

Supers are people with special abilities/powers. They might have been born with these powers, got them by accident, or acquired skills through years of practice and struggle.

Depending on their intentions, they can be classified in two broad categories : -

  1. Heroes
    2.
  2. Villains

Superheroes

Superheroes use their talents to prevent damage to the society, save lives and provide hope to the people. They're usually shown as selfless people comiitted to the betterment of the world.

Some of them are : -

  • Superman
  • Batman
  • Spiderman
  • Ironman
  • Flash
  • Captain America
  • Wolverine

Supervillains

Supervillains use their talents for personal gain, or to cause damage to the society and destroy lives for their pleasure. They're usually shown as either broken people or maniacs.

Some of them are : -

  • Joker
  • Lex Luthor
  • Doomsday
  • Darkseid
  • Loki
  • Captain Boomerang
  • Magneto
